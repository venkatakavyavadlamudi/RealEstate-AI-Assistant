# Horizon Business Park — Home Loan Partner Information

## Approved Lenders

Horizon Business Park is pre-approved for home loan disbursement by several major nationalized and private banks as well as leading housing finance companies. The current list of approved lenders is maintained by the CRM team and shared at the time of booking, since bank empanelment can change during the construction period.

## Process

1. Buyer selects a preferred lender from the approved list (or applies to an external lender, subject to that lender's own project appraisal).

2. Buyer submits KYC, income, and property documents (Agreement for Sale, RERA certificate, approved plan) to the lender.

3. Lender conducts technical and legal due diligence on the project before sanctioning the loan.

4. Disbursement is released in tranches matching Horizon Business Park's construction-linked payment plan milestones.
