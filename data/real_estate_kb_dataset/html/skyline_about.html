About Skyline Horizon Developers

Company Profile

Founded in 1998 and headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, Skyline Horizon Developers has grown into a recognized name in the region's real estate sector, with a portfolio spanning residential apartments, villas, and commercial spaces.

Leadership & Governance

Skyline Horizon Developers operates under a professional management structure with dedicated legal, engineering, and customer relationship teams overseeing each project from land acquisition through possession.

Certifications

All active projects are registered with the respective State Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA) prior to launch and marketing, in compliance with the RERA Act, 2016.
