About Urban Nest Infrastructures

Company Profile

Founded in 2011 and headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana, Urban Nest Infrastructures has grown into a recognized name in the region's real estate sector, with a portfolio spanning residential apartments, villas, and commercial spaces.

Leadership & Governance

Urban Nest Infrastructures operates under a professional management structure with dedicated legal, engineering, and customer relationship teams overseeing each project from land acquisition through possession.

Certifications

All active projects are registered with the respective State Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA) prior to launch and marketing, in compliance with the RERA Act, 2016.
